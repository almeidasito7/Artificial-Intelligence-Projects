# Standard Operating Procedure: Contractor Onboarding

**Document ID:** SOP-ONB-001  
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025  
**Last Reviewed:** March 1, 2026  
**Owner:** Human Resources Operations

## Purpose

This document outlines the standard onboarding process for all new contractors placed through our staffing services. Adherence to this process is mandatory for all regional offices.

## Pre-Start Requirements

Before a contractor's first day, the following must be completed:

1. **Background Check:** All contractors must pass a background check through our approved vendor (Sterling Check). Results must be received and cleared before the start date. Background checks typically take 3-5 business days. For healthcare placements, an additional OIG/SAM exclusion check is required.

2. **Drug Screening:** A 10-panel drug screening is required for all placements. Healthcare and government placements require an extended 12-panel screening. Results must be negative before the contractor can begin work.

3. **I-9 Verification:** Employment eligibility must be verified through E-Verify within 3 business days of the contractor's start date. The hiring coordinator is responsible for completing Section 2 of Form I-9.

4. **Client-Specific Requirements:** Some clients require additional onboarding steps such as site-specific safety training, security clearance verification, or client-managed orientation sessions. These are documented in the client profile within Job Diva.

## Day-One Checklist

On the contractor's first day, the following must be completed:

- Issue contractor ID badge and building access credentials
- Provide IT equipment as specified in the placement order (if applicable)
- Complete orientation covering company policies, timekeeping procedures, and emergency contacts
- Ensure the contractor has access to the appropriate VMS platform for timesheet submission
- Introduce the contractor to their on-site supervisor and team

## Timesheet Setup

All contractors must be set up in the client's VMS platform (Fieldglass, Beeline, VNDLY, Coupa, or IQNavigator) within 24 hours of their start date. The staffing coordinator must:

- Create the worker profile in the VMS
- Link the worker to the correct cost center and billing codes
- Verify the bill rate and pay rate match the placement agreement
- Send the contractor login instructions and a timesheet submission guide

## Compliance Tracking

The compliance team conducts a 30-day audit of all new placements to verify:

- All pre-start requirements were completed on time
- Timesheet submission has begun and is accurate
- The contractor has completed all required client-specific training
- No compliance gaps exist in the onboarding documentation

Non-compliant placements are flagged for immediate remediation. Repeated compliance failures by a regional office trigger a formal review process.

## Exceptions

Exceptions to this SOP (such as expedited starts without completed background checks) require written approval from the Regional VP and must be documented in the placement file with a risk acknowledgment form.
